Network Data for Offshore Incorporations Between Hong Kong, China and the Other International Financial Centres

These edges look at how many different offshore incorporations China and Hong Kong facilitated for other financial centres. For example, how many offshore entities did Hong Kong corporate secretarial firms and other service providers help offshore persons set up in the Cayman Islands? We look at how these links evolved comparing and contrasting Hong Kong and the Mainland.

Time Series on Offshore Incorporations in "Comparator" Jurisdictions

These data present offshore incorporations, by year, for China, Hong Kong, the Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan and the UK. We provide the Statistica sheet only - which should be easy enough to open in other software.
